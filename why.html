Nostalgia.

Well, partially. If we're honest, the internet I grew up with was a bit of a noisy mess, overpopulated with dancing gifs.

The actual reasons for the minimal websites are:

One - I like it. The most obvious reason. I find it calming. And silent.

Two - I know, more or less, what is here. I'm know very little HTML, but what is here I generally understand. Sure, for full-blown profesionnal website, there are tools like Hugo and Jekyll. But for a small one like this, I enjoy working on it more using pure HTML.

Three - Maybe because it is this direct interaction with HTML and CSS, but it feels more unmediated and human. An in the age of the rampant auto-completion, I find it just nicer.
